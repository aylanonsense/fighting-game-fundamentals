What is a fighting game?

A video game in which two players each control a character in a 2D plane. Players win by reducing their opponent to zero health through damaging moves.

Parts of a move

We can separate any move into three parts

Startup: before the first hitbox

Active: frames with a hitbox

Recovery: after the final hitbox

Parts of a move

When you use a move you are locked into that choice! You can't block, jump, or do anything else until it ends

During a move's startup and recovery you are a sitting duck!

What makes a move good?

What makes a move good?

Few startup frames

(less time for your opponent to react)

What makes a move good?

Few recovery frames

(less time you are a vulnerable)

What makes a move good?

Big hitboxes

(more likely to hit your opponent)

What makes a move good?

Ranged hitboxes

(hit your opponent from farther away)

What makes a move good?

Disjointed hitboxes

(less likely for your hurtboxes to be hit)

What makes a move good?

Lots of damage!

(more damage = win faster)

Strategy

Strategy

There are three big situations you should understand:

  • Defending yourself
  • Attacking your opponent
  • The "neutral game"

Defending yourself

Situation: The opponent is hitting you! :(

Goal: block until you find an opening

Defending yourself

Whenever you get hit (blocked or not), you slide a bit

They can't keep attacking forever!

Defending yourself

A throw is type of attack where your opponent grabs and tosses you. All throws are unblockable

Defending yourself

Throws have very short range. If the oppoent moves in close for a throw, don't block, hit them!

Defending yourself

The fundamental rock-paper-scissors of fighting games:

Defending yourself

If you fail to block, your opponent can combo you (hit you with multiple attacks) and there's nothing you can do

All you can do is avoid getting hit in the first place

Defending yourself

  • Just keep blocking
  • Hit em if they try to throw you
  • If you fail to block they can combo you--don't sweat it

Attacking your opponent

Situation: you are hitting your opponent! :D

Goal: get past their defenses and deal damage

Attacking your opponent

A combo is a series of attacks that are guaranteed to hit provided the first attack is unblocked
